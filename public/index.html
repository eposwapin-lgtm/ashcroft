Why Ashcroft

Old-world joinery, modern lead times.

Custom hardwood used to mean a year-long wait and a five-figure quote. We re-engineered our workshop so you can configure, approve, and receive a heirloom-grade piece in 4–6 weeks.

Mortise-and-tenon joinery. No staples, no MDF, no plastic — every joint is cut, fitted, and glued by a craftsperson whose name is signed on the underside of your piece.

Northeast American hardwood. White oak from the Blue Ridge, walnut from Tennessee, cherry from Kentucky — all responsibly harvested and kiln-dried on site.

4–6 week delivery. Configure size, wood, and finish online, lock in your slot, and we'll deliver and place it in your room with two-person white-glove service.

Hand-rubbed natural finishes. Plant-based hardwax oils, zero VOCs — safe for kids, pets, and the people who built it.

Our Studio

A small workshop on Riverside Drive.

Ashcroft & Co. started in 2017 when two cabinetmakers — Henry Ashcroft and Clara Boone — got tired of seeing beautiful Appalachian oak disappear into disposable furniture. They opened a 4,200 sq ft shop on Riverside Drive with one rule: nothing leaves the floor unless it can be repaired by a grandchild.

Nine years and 8,600 pieces later, that rule still holds. Every dining table, bed frame, sideboard, and bench is signed, dated, and registered to its original owner so we can service it for life.
